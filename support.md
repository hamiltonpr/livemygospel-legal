# Live My Gospel — Support

**Last updated:** May 22, 2026

Live My Gospel is a planner app for weekly goals, calendar events, and relationship tracking. Version 1.0 includes **Home**, **Planner**, **People**, and **Settings**. An internet connection is required to sign in and sync your data.

## Sign in

You can sign in with:

- **Continue with Google**, or  
- **Email and password** (sign in or create an account)

If you created an account with email, you may need to enter a **confirmation code** from your inbox before your first sign-in.

**Tips:**

- Check spam/junk for confirmation or password-reset mail.
- Make sure you have a working internet connection.
- If sign-in fails after an app update, try **Sign out** (if you are still signed in), force-quit the app, reopen it, and sign in again.

## Delete your account

You can permanently delete your account **inside the app**—no email required.

1. Open **Settings** (bottom tab).
2. Scroll to **Account**.
3. Tap **Delete Account** and confirm.

This removes your sign-in and your app data from our servers. It cannot be undone.

For step-by-step details and troubleshooting, see [How to delete your account](DELETE_ACCOUNT.md) in this repo (or your hosted copy of that page).

## Common questions

### Sync or “can’t save” errors

- Confirm you are signed in.
- Check Wi‑Fi or cellular data.
- Wait a moment and try again; if it persists, sign out and sign back in.

### Calendar import (Google / ICS URL)

1. In **Settings**, use **Import from calendar URL**.
2. Paste the **Secret address in iCal format** from Google Calendar (ends in `.ics`).
3. Review categories, then import.

Google’s help article: [Get your calendar’s address](https://support.google.com/calendar/answer/37111).

If import fails, verify the URL is public/secret ICS link, not only a web page link, and try again on a stable connection.

### Weekly goals or categories look wrong

In **Settings** you can edit **Weekly Goals**, **Calendar Categories**, and **Dot Labels**, or use **Restore defaults** for built-in labels. Restoring defaults may remap custom categories to **Other**—read the confirmation text before confirming.

### People / status dots

- Add or edit people from the **People** tab.
- Change sort with **A–Z** or **By Color** on the People screen.
- Optional **auto-dot rules** are under **Settings**.

### Newsletter (Two Tacos and a Hike)

If you opted in from the welcome screen, manage that subscription on the newsletter site ([twotacosandahike.beehiiv.com](https://twotacosandahike.beehiiv.com)). Deleting your Live My Gospel account does **not** automatically unsubscribe you there.
