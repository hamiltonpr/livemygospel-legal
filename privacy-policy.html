Live My Gospel — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

Summary

Live My Gospel uses Supabase for sign-in and to store your planner and people data. The current app focuses on calendar planning, weekly goals, and relationship tracking. Social feed features are not available in this version.

Data We Collect

We collect your email address for sign-in and the records you create in the app, including calendar events, weekly goals, people contacts, status-dot settings, and optional display name if you set one in Settings.

How We Use Data

Your data is used to sign you in, sync your account across devices, and show your saved planner and people information. We do not use your data for advertising.

Sharing

Your calendar, goals, dot settings, and people records stay private to your account. We do not sell your personal information. Service providers (such as Supabase) process data only to operate the app.

Retention And Control

You can delete your account in Settings. Account deletion removes your sign-in account and your app data from our backend.

Contact

For privacy questions about Live My Gospel, contact the developer listed on the Google Play store listing for this app.
