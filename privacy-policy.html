Live My Gospel — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

Summary

Live My Gospel uses Supabase for sign-in and to store your planner and people data. The current app focuses on calendar planning, weekly goals, and relationship tracking. Social feed features are not available in version 1.0. We do not sell your personal information or use your data for third-party advertising, and we do not track you across other companies’ apps or websites for advertising.

Data We Collect

We collect your email address and account identifier for sign-in (including Sign in with Google or email and password). We store the information you enter in the app, including calendar events, weekly goals, status-dot settings, an optional display name, and People profiles. People profiles may include names, phone numbers, email addresses, mailing addresses, notes, and where you met. If you opt in to our optional newsletter, we send your email to our newsletter provider (Beehiiv) for that publication only. If photo upload features are available in your version of the app, photos you choose from your library may be stored on our servers when you attach them to a post.

How We Use Data

Your data is used to authenticate you, sync your account across devices, and provide planner and people features. Optional newsletter opt-in is used only to subscribe you to that mailing list if you turn it on. We do not use your data for third-party advertising or cross-app advertising tracking.

Sharing

Your calendar, goals, dot settings, and people records stay private to your account. We do not sell your personal information. Service providers process data only to operate the app: Supabase (hosting, database, and authentication) and, if you opt in, Beehiiv (newsletter). We do not share your planner data with advertisers.

Retention And Control

You can delete your account in Settings → Delete Account. Account deletion removes your sign-in account and your app data from our backend. Deleting your app account does not automatically unsubscribe you from the optional newsletter; manage that on the newsletter provider’s site.
