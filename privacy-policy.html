Live My Gospel — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

Summary

Live My Gospel uses Supabase for sign-in and to sync your planner and people data. The app focuses on calendar planning, weekly goals, daily tasks, and relationship tracking. The Chapter 13 social feed is not available in the current release. We do not sell your personal information, use third-party advertising, or track you across other companies’ apps or websites.

Data We Collect

We collect your email address and account identifier for sign-in (email and password, or Sign in with Google where available). We store the information you enter in the app, including calendar events, weekly goals, daily tasks, status-dot settings, an optional display name, and People profiles. People profiles may include names, phone numbers, email addresses, mailing addresses, notes, birthdays, and where you met.

If you tap Import from Contacts on a People profile, you choose a contact from your phone. We copy the fields from that contact (such as name, phone, email, address, and birthday) into a People profile on our servers. We do not upload your full address book.

If you enable reminders in Settings, the app schedules local notifications on your device for calendar events and daily tasks. Reminder text is built from your planner data on your device. We do not use a remote push notification service and do not collect a push token for delivery.

If you opt in to our optional newsletter, we send your email to our newsletter provider (Beehiiv) for that publication only. If photo upload features are available in your version of the app, photos you choose from your library may be stored on our servers when you attach them to a post.

How We Use Data

Your data is used to authenticate you, sync your account across devices, and provide planner, calendar, goals, tasks, and people features. Optional newsletter opt-in is used only to subscribe you to that mailing list if you turn it on. Local reminders are used only when you enable them. We do not use your data for third-party advertising or cross-app advertising tracking.

Sharing

Your calendar, goals, tasks, dot settings, and people records stay private to your account. We do not sell your personal information. Service providers process data only to operate the app: Supabase (hosting, database, and authentication) and, if you opt in, Beehiiv (newsletter). We do not share your planner data with advertisers.

Retention And Control

You can delete your account in Settings → Delete Account. Account deletion removes your sign-in account and your app data from our backend. Deleting your app account does not automatically unsubscribe you from the optional newsletter; manage that on the newsletter provider’s site.
